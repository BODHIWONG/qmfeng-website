# Qi Men Course Hierarchy

## Level 1 — Foundation Practical Course
- Duration: 2 days (Saturday and Sunday)
- Fee: S$990 per participant
- Status: weekly public enrolment
- Purpose: establish the core chart-reading framework and complete guided introductory case practice
- Boundary: does not represent mastery of the complete Qi Men system

## Level 2 — Intermediate Course
- Duration and fee: to be announced
- Entry: Foundation completion or equivalent ability, subject to review
- Purpose: strengthen useful-god selection, multi-palace analysis, timing and structured case interpretation

## Level 3 — Advanced Course
- Duration and fee: to be announced
- Entry: Intermediate completion or equivalent ability, subject to review
- Purpose: complex chart synthesis, business and major-decision cases, scenario comparison and supervised reasoning

## Level 4 — Disciple Programme
- Format: long-term mentorship, application-based
- Entry: application and assessment only
- Purpose: disciplined case submission, correction, ethics and deeper applied development

The Foundation Course must be presented as a complete two-day practical introduction, not as the entire Qi Men curriculum. Later levels must remain visibly distinct in purpose, entry requirement, depth and delivery format.
