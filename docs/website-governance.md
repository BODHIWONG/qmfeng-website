# Qimen Strategy Website Governance

This document is the approved source of truth for public website structure and change control.

## Change control

- Do not change public website copy, pricing, course structure, routes or functionality without the owner's explicit approval.
- All approved changes must be made on a branch, checked through a Vercel preview and merged to `main` only after the checks pass.
- `main` is the production source of truth for the Vercel project serving `www.qmfeng.com`.

## Brand naming

- Public brand: `Qimen Strategy｜启明遁甲决策智库`
- Short form where space is limited: `Qimen Strategy｜启明遁甲`
- Founder: `Huang Qiming｜黄启明`
- Public title: `Founder & Strategic Decision Advisor`
- Do not use `Master`, `大师`, `Qiming Feng Shui`, `奇明风水` or `奇明風水` as current public brand or founder titles.
- Legal and payment name only: `QIMING FENG SHUI WISDOM PTE. LTD.`

## Public pricing policy

- Qi Men Dun Jia Foundation Course: `S$990 per participant`
- Focused single-decision consultation: `From S$396`, with the final fee confirmed by scope and complexity.
- Complex, multi-party, enterprise and ongoing advisory: `By application / scope-based proposal`.
- Do not publish legacy fixed advisory prices such as S$980, S$2,800, S$9,800 or S$25,000 on public legacy pages.
- Retail-product prices are not part of the core advisory pricing system. The old public product catalogue is retired until a new approved page is produced.
