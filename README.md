## Current Business Hierarchy

The website follows a course-led business funnel.

### 1. Qimen Strategy Academy — Primary Entry

- Qi Men Dun Jia Foundation Course｜奇门遁甲基础课程
- Regular Saturday–Sunday intakes from 19 September 2026
- Course fee: S$990 per participant
- Dedicated registration, PayNow, attendance and transfer policies
- Courses are the main trust-building and client-entry pathway

The course helps learners understand the Qimen Strategy framework, reading sequence, practical cases and professional boundaries. It is the first public-facing business priority.

### 2. Qimen Strategy Advisory — Advanced Pathway

- Founder Business Advisory｜创业老板事业陪跑
- Wealth Cycle & Investment Decision Support｜财富周期与投资决策辅助
- Executive Career Transition Advisory｜高管事业转型与关键关系规划
- Relationship & Major Life Decision Support｜个人情感与重大决策支持

Advisory is the second business engine. It is intended for founders, executives and individuals facing real business bottlenecks, investment, people, transition or major decision issues. Course learners and other qualified clients may progress into advisory when deeper support is appropriate.

Advisory content must remain professional, evidence-aware and clearly bounded. Do not promise revenue, profit, investment return, promotion, reconciliation or other specific outcomes. Financial, legal, tax, medical, psychological and other regulated matters must be referred to appropriately qualified professionals.
